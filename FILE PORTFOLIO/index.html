Projects

IMPLEMENTING COOLING RODS IN NUCLEAR POWER PLANTS

This project utilizes Natural Language Processing (NLP) techniques to automate the scheduling and reminders for cooling rod replacements in nuclear reactors. By analyzing textual data from maintenance logs and operational reports, the system identifies key indicators related to cooling rod lifespan and deterioration. It employs machine learning algorithms to predict optimal replacement times based on historical data and current operational conditions. The implementation aims to enhance operational efficiency, ensure regulatory compliance, and minimize downtime by proactively managing cooling rod maintenance schedules through automated notifications and alerts.

CANTEEN MANAGEMENT SYSTEM

The Canteen Management System (CMS) is a comprehensive solution designed to streamline and automate the operations of canteens or cafeterias within organizations. It facilitates efficient management of food ordering, menu planning, inventory control, and customer billing. The system enables users to place orders electronically, monitor stock levels in real-time, and generate reports for analysis and decision-making. By integrating digital payment options and user-friendly interfaces, CMS enhances convenience for both canteen staff and customers, optimizing service delivery and enhancing overall operational efficiency.
